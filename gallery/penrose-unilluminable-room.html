Penrose unilluminable room

Contributors: Vincent Fan, Yi-Ting Tu

The Penrose unilluminable room is constructed from elliptic, circular, and linear mirrors. It has the interesting property that a point source (green) within the room will not shine the entire room, no matter where you put it. You can drag the green point to move the source.
